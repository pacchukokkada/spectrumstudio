About Us


Taking photography and travel as a hobby to a full time career option, we are a team of enthusiastic and passionate driven creators based in Dharmasthala, Dakshina Kannada. We started our venture in 2018 and have since worked on over a hundred engagements, wedding ceremonies, pre and post wedding shoots, baby showers, kid and model photography and even short movies. Photographers, videographers, editors, cinematographers, drone operators we have them all! We are constantly on the hunt to find new ways to tell your story and are open to working in any location across india!. Our goal is to capture and create your special day to last a lifetime and give u a serious sense of nostalgia.
